About Me

Creative Explorer & Builder

I'm Harvie, a 21-year-old creative explorer based in Passi City, Iloilo. I believe in living life at the intersection of structure and spontaneous expression. Whether shaping soundwaves in my home studio, capturing transient moments on the streets with my camera, or finding routes up rock walls, I approach every passion with dedication and care.

Through music, photography, and climbing, I seek to capture the textures of the world around us. This portfolio is a digital gallery of my creative work, journals, and adventures.
